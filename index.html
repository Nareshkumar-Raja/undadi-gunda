“ABOUT US”

Our journey in making fresh & authentic masala powders.

Undadi Gunda was started with a simple mission — to bring back the traditional, homemade flavours of Karnataka. Every masala is prepared in small batches using hand-picked spices sourced directly from trusted farmers. We grind fresh only after receiving an order, ensuring maximum aroma, purity and shelf life.

  • Made using premium-quality spices with zero artificial additives.

  • Freshly ground every week for the most authentic flavour.
